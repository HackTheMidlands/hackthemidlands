School Outreach Scheme

What is the HackTheMidlands School Outreach Scheme?

We want to get more students into hackathons!

As some of us have recently finished school, we know the tech opportunities aren't brilliant. We want to make more students aware of these opportunities, helping them get into programming and making cool projects!

These opportunities either exist, aren't known about, or are intimidating to those just getting into programming. We want to fix that by giving students the support and guidance they need to learn!

What can we do?

We can offer a variety of sessions.
